Who we are?

We are an independent, student-driven research initiative in India focused on astrobiology — exploring life in extreme environments, biosignatures, laboratory simulations and outreach for schools and universities.

  • Mentorship by leading scientists from top institutions
  • Hands-on projects and modular training
  • Outreach & community programs across India

What we do?

Research, training and outreach bridging biology, geology and planetary science.

Planetary Analogs

Field and laboratory work on extreme-habitability sites — deserts, salt pans and high-altitude lakes.

Training & Outreach

Workshops, online modules and school outreach to build capacity in space life sciences across India.

Lab Simulations

Simulating planetary conditions, biosignature detection and microbial survivability experiments.

Programs & Opportunities

project-based mentorships and open research tasks for undergraduate and master students.

Research Program

8–12 week guided project with laboratory & field components.

Online Training Modules

Self-paced courses in planetary environments, extremophiles and astrobiology methods.

Collaborative Projects

Partnered projects with universities and research labs.
